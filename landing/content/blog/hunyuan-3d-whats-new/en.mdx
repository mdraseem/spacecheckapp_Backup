# September 2025: Tencent’s New Powerhouse

In September 2025, the Chinese tech giant Tencent unveiled a major upgrade to the Hunyuan model — the largest open-source AI 3D model generator available today. If you've ever wanted to **convert an image to a 3D model** with professional-grade quality, this is the release to pay attention to. While the technology under the hood is complex, we've broken down exactly what makes this version a game-changer.

# What’s New?

* **Understanding Object Logic:** One of the biggest pain points in previous models was structural consistency. Hunyuan 3.0 uses a **"rough-to-fine"** approach—first sculpting the fundamental geometry before layering on micro-details.
* **Higher Fidelity:** Resolution has been bumped up to **1536³**, making these models high-quality enough for professional use in game development and VR.
* **Multimodal Input:** Previously, you were limited to a single **image to 3D** pipeline. Now, you can provide text prompts alongside your photo to give the model more context, resulting in significantly more precise 3D assets.

# Hunyuan 3D 3.0

In my opinion, Tencent's latest release is the current state-of-the-art for **image to 3D model** generation. It might not be the absolute fastest on the market, but we are seeing a clear shift from "large" models to "smart" ones—which is a fantastic sign for anyone looking to **convert a photo to a 3D model** quickly and accurately.

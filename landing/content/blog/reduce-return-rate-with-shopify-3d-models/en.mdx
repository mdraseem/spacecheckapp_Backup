# Introduction

When a customer wants to "try" a product before buying, there is a traditional, manual approach: the supplier lists dimensions and specs in the description, and the customer uses a tape measure to see if it fits. 

But there is a much more effective way to bridge the gap between expectation and reality: **Augmented Reality (AR).**

# Why AR Helps?

Studies from Wendrich Digital Agency show that **35% of all e-commerce returns** occur due to product misalignment. 3D models fix this "visualization gap." 

From a growth perspective, if you are selling a product, you want to optimize your conversion rate—and having a 3D model is one of the most effective ways to do it.

# Shopify vs 3D & AR

* **On Mobile:** Shopify utilizes WebAR technology, allowing users to not only view the 3D model but also "place" the object into their physical world.

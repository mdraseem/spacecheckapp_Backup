# SpaceCheck.app — Marketing Plan: Polish Furniture Market

## Target Market

Poland is Europe's 2nd largest furniture exporter with 28,000+ companies and a $2.28B e-commerce furniture segment (growing 10-15% YoY). Online share reached ~45% in 2024. Most manufacturers still rely on static product photos — AR adoption is near zero.

---

## Priority Target Companies (3 Tiers)

### Tier 1 — Flagship Clients (high impact, case-study potential)

| Company | Type | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Black Red White** | Manufacturer + Retailer (80+ showrooms) | Market leader, 20% share, omnichannel — biggest showcase win |
| **Meble VOX** | Manufacturer + Retailer (150+ stores) | Already has 3D configurator (VOXBOX) — AR is natural next step |
| **Agata Meble** | Multi-brand Retailer (32 showrooms, 1.6M/mo visits) | High-traffic e-commerce, serves 250+ manufacturers |
| **Kler** | Premium Manufacturer | Luxury sofas/kitchens, high AOV — AR reduces buyer hesitation |
| **Tylko** | D2C Startup (custom shelving, $33M funded) | Tech-native, online-only, already has high return costs |
| **Stolkar** | Kitchen & Bathroom Manufacturer | Kitchen visualization = killer AR use case, B2B+B2C |

### Tier 2 — Volume Manufacturers

**Forte, Szynaka, Bodzio, Gala Collezione, Meble Wójcik, Halmar, Nobonobo, Selsey**

Large catalogs, active e-commerce, modular/configurable products — ideal for batch AR conversion.

### Tier 3 — Platforms & Niche

**Mirjan24** (70K+ products online), **Twojemeble.pl** / **Domowanie.pl** (aggregators reaching dozens of producers), **Nowy Styl / Profim** (office B2B), **Komandor** (custom wardrobes), **JYSK Poland** (300+ stores).

---

## Value Proposition (Polish Market Messaging)

> **"Zamień zdjęcia produktów w sprzedaż AR — bez studia 3D, bez programistów"**
> *(Turn product photos into AR sales — no 3D studio, no developers)*

**Key proof points for outreach:**

- 40% higher conversion with AR visualization
- 30% fewer returns (furniture size/color mismatch)
- 2-5 min per model, no special equipment
- Works on iOS + Android without app download
- Auto-generated QR posters for physical showrooms
- From $49/month (Growth plan)

## Recommended First Moves

1. **Pick 3 companies from Tier 1** — scrape 5 product photos from each, generate AR models, send as cold outreach: *"We built AR for your [product name] — scan this QR to see it in your room"*
2. **Publish Polish landing page** (already have PL localization) with `.pl` domain or `/pl` path optimized for "meble AR", "wirtualna aranżacja mebli"
3. **Target Meble VOX first** — they already invested in 3D (VOXBOX), so they understand the value; SpaceCheck is faster and cheaper than maintaining in-house
4. **Partner with Shoper.pl** (dominant Polish e-commerce platform for SMBs) — create a SpaceCheck plugin for their app marketplace to reach thousands of small furniture sellers at once

*This plan prioritizes proof over promises — generate real AR models from target companies' products and let the demo sell itself.*
